Headline: New DOOM Eternal

QuakeCon is returning to the Gaylord Texan Resort and Convention Center in Dallas, Texas. This year’s event – dubbed QuakeCon: Year of DOOM in honor of DOOM’s 25th anniversary – will run from July 25 through July 28 and feature a host of panels, events, activities and, of course, North America’s largest BYOC area.
